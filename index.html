Our Services

Med Arena

MED ARENA is specifically designed for medical admission test preparation of English Medium (A Level) students only. Usually after passing A level, most students tend to let go off their dreams to study medicine for either of two reasons:

  • Overseas Med schools are very expensive
  • Admission Test for local medical universities are very competitive

We decided to address the second reason. The English Medium students find the admission test difficult mainly due to the fact that the H.S.C. syllabus is very much different from A level syllabus. We designed MED ARENA in a way where students can bridge the gap between two syllabi.

Admission Arena

From finding a university to actually stepping foot on the campus we will be there at your service
